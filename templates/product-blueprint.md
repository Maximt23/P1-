# Product Blueprint Template

This template serves as the standard blueprint for defining new white-label products or operational automations within the Personal AI Startup Factory. It ensures consistency, clarity, and comprehensive planning for each product from conception to deployment.

## 1. Product Overview
-   **Product Name:** [Concise, descriptive name of the product]
-   **Product ID:** [Unique identifier for the product, e.g., `PAI-PRD-001`]
-   **Purpose/Problem Solved:** [What problem does this product solve? What is its core value proposition?]
-   **Target Audience:** [Who are the primary users or customers for this product?]
-   **Key Features & Functionality:** [List the essential features and capabilities of the product.]
-   **Desired Outcome/KPIs:** [How will the success of this product be measured?]

## 2. Technical Architecture
-   **Core Components:** [Outline the main architectural components, e.g., shared core modules, specific project agents, data stores.]
-   **Technology Stack:** [List primary programming languages, frameworks, databases, and key libraries.]
-   **Integration Points:** [Describe how this product integrates with other systems, APIs, or shared services.]
-   **Scalability & Performance Goals:** [Define non-functional requirements related to scalability, performance, and reliability.]
-   **Security Considerations:** [Outline key security requirements and planned measures.]

## 3. Agent System & Workflow
-   **Primary Project Agent:** [Reference to the dedicated project agent, e.g., `projects/product-name/agents/product-agent.md`]
-   **Involved Shared Agents:** [List any shared agents (`git-manager`, `docs-writer`, `qa-agent`, `refactorer`) that will be actively involved in this product's lifecycle and their specific roles.]
-   **Development Workflow:** [Outline the high-level steps for development, from tasking to QA and deployment.]
-   **Memory Integration:** [How will project-specific learnings for this product be captured in `projects/product-name/memory.md`?]

## 4. Operational & Deployment
-   **Deployment Strategy:** [How will the product be deployed (e.g., cloud, on-premise, containerized)?]
-   **Monitoring & Logging:** [Describe planned monitoring, logging, and alerting mechanisms.]
-   **Support & Maintenance:** [Outline considerations for ongoing support, maintenance, and updates.]
-   **Packaging Requirements:** [Specific requirements for packaging this product for white-label delivery.]
-   **Handoff Procedures:** [Outline the steps for handing off the completed product to a client or deployment team.]

## 5. Cost & Efficiency
-   **Estimated Token Usage:** [Initial estimates for token usage during development and operation, and strategies for optimization.]
-   **Cost-Control Measures:** [Specific measures to manage development and operational costs.]
-   **Efficiency Targets:** [Goals for minimizing redundant work, maximizing reuse, and accelerating development cycles.]

## 6. QA & Acceptance Criteria
-   **Key QA Areas:** [Outline the critical areas for quality assurance and testing.]
-   **Acceptance Criteria:** [Define the specific criteria that must be met for this product to be considered complete and ready for launch/delivery.]
-   **Testing Strategy:** [Briefly describe the testing approach (e.g., unit, integration, end-to-end testing).]

## 7. Versioning & Documentation
-   **Versioning Strategy:** [How will product versions be managed?]
-   **Documentation Plan:** [Outline the required documentation (user manuals, API docs, deployment guides, etc.) and responsibilities for creating it.]

## 8. Business & Marketing (if applicable)
-   **Value Proposition for Clients:** [How will this product be positioned to clients?]
-   **Pricing Strategy:** [Basic pricing considerations for white-labeling.]
