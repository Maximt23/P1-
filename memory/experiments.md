# Experiments Log

**Purpose:** To track experiments and trials, compare approaches, and keep a record of what was tested and what was learned to guide future development and decision-making.

## Entries:

### Experiment: (YYYY-MM-DD) [Brief, descriptive title of the experiment]
-   **Hypothesis:** What was the expected outcome or learning from this experiment?
-   **Setup:** Describe how the experiment was designed and executed (e.g., specific prompts, agent configurations, code variations).
-   **Result:** What were the observed outcomes, data, or behaviors?
-   **Interpretation:** What do the results mean? Was the hypothesis supported? What new insights were gained?
-   **Next step:** What actions should be taken based on these learnings (e.g., pursue this approach, discard it, conduct further experiments)?
