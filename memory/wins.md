# Wins Log

**Purpose:** To record successful patterns, capture workflows or prompts that worked, and identify reusable tactics to improve future execution quality and efficiency.

## Entries:

### Win: (YYYY-MM-DD) [Brief, descriptive title of the successful outcome]
-   **Context:** What was the situation or goal when this success was achieved?
-   **What worked:** Describe the approach, workflow, or code that led to the successful outcome.
-   **Why it worked:** Explain the key factors or principles that contributed to this success.
-   **Reuse guidance:** How can this pattern or tactic be reapplied in future situations?
-   **Conditions where it applies:** Under what circumstances is this win most relevant or effective?
